# User Journey

Version: 2.0.0-alpha

Status: Frozen

---

# Day 1

The user creates a new employee.

Example:

aw init TemuPilot

The employee knows nothing.

---

# Day 2

The user starts training.

Upload:

- documents
- screenshots
- SOP
- videos
- business rules

The employee begins to understand the business.

---

# Day 7

The employee can answer questions.

Example:

Why did sales drop yesterday?

Which products are growing?

What should I replenish?

---

# Day 30

The employee starts making suggestions.

Example:

Three potential best-selling products found.

Supplier price increased.

Inventory risk detected.

Advertising budget should be reduced.

---

# Day 90

The employee performs work independently.

Example:

Collect data.

Analyze products.

Generate reports.

Prepare product listings.

Wait for approval.

---

# Day 180

The employee collaborates with other employees.

Example:

TemuPilot

↓

asks

↓

FinancialAssistant

↓

asks

↓

WarehouseManager

↓

asks

↓

MarketingAssistant

The user manages a team instead of individual tasks.

---

# Final Experience

The user no longer operates software.

The user manages AI employees.
